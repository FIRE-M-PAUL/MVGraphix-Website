When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information:

2.2 Communication

  • Sending project updates and status notifications

  • Sending marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Providing customer service and technical support

2.3 Website Improvement

2.4 Legal and Security

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

5.1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide and improve our services. They enable us to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements
  • Improve website functionality and performance

5.2 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for advertising and marketing purposes

5.3 Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.
